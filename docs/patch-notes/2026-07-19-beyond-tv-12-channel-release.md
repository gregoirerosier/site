# Beyond TV 12-channel release

- Expands the featured lineup from eight to twelve channels.
- Adds Beyond Comedy, Beyond Mystery, Beyond After Dark, and Beyond Family.
- Adds 96 scheduled three-hour programming blocks across the twelve-channel guide.
- Triages the 23-title owner watchlist into comedy, mystery, supernatural, and family programming.
- Adds direct playback for the owner-verified Sister Act 2 and Bring It On Archive files.
- Adds 152 individually named episodes of R. L. Stine’s The Haunting Hour.
- Adds four Winnie the Pooh Storybook Classics as a selectable story library.
- Promotes 18 previously selected watchlist candidates with exact Archive identifiers to owner-verified embedded playback.
- Keeps `Are You Afraid of the Dark?` pending because it has an Archive search link rather than one exact item identifier.
- Makes the home channel grid data-driven and limits the Live TV page to the twelve featured channels.
- Adds owner-verified Archive iframe playback for 21 Jump Street, The Princess Bride, Matilda, The Jungle Book VHS edition, That ’70s Show Seasons 1 and 3, The Hateful Eight Extended Version, and Aladdin VHS edition.
