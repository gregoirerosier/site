# World English Bible source

The bundled `engwebp_vpl.txt` is the World English Bible, Protestant edition (WEBP), downloaded unchanged from eBible.org.

- Source: https://ebible.org/details.php?id=engwebp
- Download: https://ebible.org/Scriptures/engwebp_vpl.zip
- Status: Public domain
- Verses: 31,103

The World English Bible name is used only to identify the unchanged public-domain translation supplied by eBible.org.
